# 001. Drucker Sources

This source note supports [reports/en/001_drucker_management_kpi.md](https://github.com/kuroam/hss-observation-reports/blob/main/reports/en/001_drucker_management_kpi.md).

This note does not provide an exhaustive summary of the sources.

It provides source anchors for the context used in the observation report.

This is not a comprehensive bibliography.

The sources listed here are not foundations or evidence for HSS.

They are also not used to definitively determine Drucker’s intent through HSS.

They are used to confirm the surrounding contexts in which Drucker’s management, objectives, MBO, measurement, KPIs, and management practice are discussed.

## HSS-side use

In Report 001, HSS does not ask whether Drucker “really meant KPI.”

Instead, it observes a connection structure in which Drucker-like questions about mission, customer, customer value, results, and planning can be routed into practical management forms such as objectives, MBO, KPIs, evaluation formats, and dashboards.

The source anchors are used to confirm that Drucker, management by objectives, measurement, and KPI-related contexts are externally recognizable.

They are not used as proof of HSS.

The report observes how Drucker-related questions and management concepts may undergo compression into objectives, MBO, KPIs, evaluation, dashboards, and measurement formats.

It also observes how those concepts may be routed into a processing form before HSS is applied as a tentative observation axis.
